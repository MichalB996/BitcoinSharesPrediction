The primary advice ensures compliance with the foundations set by the FATF as described within the steering for crypto property and crypto asset service suppliers (CASPs) that the cash laundering watchdog printed in June final 12 months. The FATF has since been actively imposing its requirements on member international locations. CASPs embrace crypto buying and selling platforms, ATMs, token issuers, funds and derivatives service suppliers, custodial wallets, and different custodial companies. The coverage paper provides:
