TITLE: The History Behind Warren Buffett's Berkshire Hathaway
URL:   https://www.thestreet.com/video/warren-buffetts-berkshire-hathaway-history

Billionaire Warren Buffett needs no introduction as he is one of the wealthiest people on this planet. He made a fortune running his company Berkshire Hathaway (BRK.A) - Get Report.

As of February 2020, he's worth $85 billion.

He is regarded as one of the most successful investors of all time. He is often referred as "Oracle of Omaha" as his company Berkshire Hathaway is headquartered in Omaha, Nebraska.

Berkshire Hathaway owns more than 60 companies, including Benjamin Moore, Geico and more.

You might find it interesting to know that Berkshire Hathaway is originally a textile manufacturer. Buffett's investing group took it over in 1966, and the rest, as they say, is history.

Today, its market cap is valued at over $500 billion.
