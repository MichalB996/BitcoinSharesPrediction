TITLE: Binance Hikes Leverage to 125x for Launch of Bitcoin-Tether Futures
URL:   https://sunriseread.com/binance-hikes-leverage-to-125x-for-launch-of-bitcoin-tether-futures/88444/

Binance’s futures trade is elevating leverage to 125x with the launch of bitcoin (BTC)/tether (USDT) contracts right now.

The cryptocurrency trade first introduced its futures platform in early July, on the time hinting it might enable 20x leverage. Now, futures merchants can now select leveraged positions between 1x and 125x, it mentioned Friday.

“We've seen a rise in institutional participation in buying and selling … And they're flocking to Binance Futures,” mentioned Binance CEO Changpeng “CZ” Zhao in an announcement. “The market has been demanding a product with superior stability and efficiency; now we're offering one.”

Max leverage positions on Binance now exceed crypto trade BitMEX’s futures leverage positions, which high off at 100x for some contracts.

Famous economist and crypto skeptic Nouriel Roubini has beforehand argued that, in offering such excessive leverage, exchanges are exposing merchants to an excessive amount of danger.

Binance mentioned its BTC futures contract offers a built-in hedging instrument to assist merchants handle that danger. Additional, the trade mentioned it’s offering an “insurance coverage fund” (the agency’s citation marks) that “helps to restrict the possibilities of auto-deleverage.”

The announcement comes amidst tightening competitors within the bitcoin futures area. Intercontinental Trade’s Bakkt bitcoin futures trade launched this September. Though the trade’s preliminary quantity disenchanted critics, the trade has already performed its first block commerce for two institutional buyers.
